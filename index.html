Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the building calculators?

Our building calculators use data collected from various players with different buffs. While we strive for accuracy, actual in-game values might vary slightly. Consider the results as a good estimate for planning purposes.

What are the NAP rules?

NAP (Non-Aggression Pact) rules govern peaceful interactions between alliances. They include guidelines on attacking other players, resource tiles, and consequences for violations. Check out our detailed NAP page for a complete breakdown.

How do I optimize my hero formations?

Use our Formation Builder tool to create balanced hero teams. For best results, consider hero types (Tank, Air, Missile), their positions (front vs back), and synergy bonuses. Matching types gives additional attack bonuses.

What's the most efficient way to level up heroes?

Focus on one squad type (Tank, Air, or Missile) initially. Prioritize leveling up your strongest UR heroes first, particularly damage dealers in the back row. Use our Hero EXP calculator to plan level milestones efficiently.
